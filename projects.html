Restoring Hope (Class Choice Award)

2-D narrative-driven pixel using Unity and C#

Play as a pretty princess fixing up her castle with her magic wand. The more she fixes, the more... things go wrong? Explore her castle and investigate strange discrepancies to learn the truth behind the sparkles and magic.

I conceptualized and implemented the game's interactive puzzles. I also integrated many of the environmental assets to ensure cohesive game design. This project was the work of a team of 9 students, including developers, artists, and sound designers.

Ultimate

Website to play Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe with a friend! Made using React, JavaScript, CSS, TypeScript, and HTML

Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe is a complex version of the classic game with nine 3x3 boards to create an ultimate large board. Each of your moves will determine where your opponent can play next. Wins on the smaller boards count as moves on the larger board. Win the whole game by getting 3-in-a-row on the large board!

I developed this interactive web application using React framework. I also incorporated React Hooks (state management) to handle game logic such as tracking and updating game state across multiple boards, handling tie conditions, and indicating the next allowed moves.

Artifacts of the Past

2D Puzzle Platformer using Unity and C#

The amenders were immortal beings that had the power to travel in time and change past events. However, the strain of time-traveling took its toll and now you are the only one left. Suddenly, your world devolves into chaos as sudden changes begin to appear. You recognize this as the work of a time-traveler. Switch between the past and present to fix your world and fight against whoever is responsible.

I worked on this project with a group of 4 students. I designed many of the puzzles and crafted the levels. I also implemented assets for characters, enemies, and the environment.
