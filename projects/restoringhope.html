Restoring Hope (Class Choice Award)

2-D narrative-driven puzzle game about a young girl fixing up her castle.

Unity C#
Role: Puzzle Lead, UI Designer, Developer
Team Size: 9 people
Release: 2024
Play

Play as a pretty princess fixing up her castle with her magic wand. The more she fixes, the more... things go wrong? Explore her castle and investigate strange discrepancies to learn the truth behind the sparkles and magic.

Responsibilities

Puzzle Lead, UI Designer, Developer

  • Led puzzle design and development by conceptualizing and implementing several core puzzle, including the lock, TV signal, sliding, and wire puzzles.
  • Organized game flow, defining puzzle locations and conditions, collectibles, and item interactions.
  • Updated narrative elements and created puzzles to support story progression.
  • Worked closely with artists to implement UI elements, room layouts, animations, and collectibles.
  • Prepared promotional and release assets.
