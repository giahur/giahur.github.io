Restoring Hope (Class Choice Award)

Play

2-D narrative-driven pixel puzzle game using Unity and C#

Play as a pretty princess fixing up her castle with her magic wand. The more she fixes, the more... things go wrong? Explore her castle and investigate strange discrepancies to learn the truth behind the sparkles and magic.

This project was the work of a team of 9 students, including developers, artists, and sound designers. We worked on this project for 15 weeks with weekly sprints. I led puzzle development, conceptualizing and implementing many of the game's interactive puzzles.
