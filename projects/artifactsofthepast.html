Artifacts of the Past

Play

2D Puzzle Platformer using Unity and C#

For years you thought you were the last time traveler left. But suddenly, someone starts wreaking havoc in the past and it's up to you to stop them. Switch between the past and present to avoid obstacles, fight enemies, and solve puzzles to face whoever is responsible.

I worked on this project with a group of 4 students over the course of 4 weeks. I designed many of the puzzles and crafted the levels. I also implemented assets for characters, enemies, and the environment.
