WhenIsGreat

Browser Application designed to streamline scheduling group meetings. Built with React, Spring Boot, MySQL, Java, CSS, HTML, JavaScript, and OAuth authentication

This project was completed with a group of 4 students in 12 weeks. The primary objective of this project was to develop a user-friendly and visually appealing application that finds common availabilities with Google Calendar integration. Users can log in with their Google account and create, edit, view and share events.
