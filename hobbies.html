Hobbies

Besides designing games and websites, I also enjoy...

Video Games

Since I was younger, my family and I have played Super Mario Bros., Mario Kart, and Just Dance on the Wii. I also love classic DS games like Nintendogs, Luigi's Mansion, and Animal Crossing.

With my friends, I enjoy co-op games including Minecraft, We Were Here, Death Road to Canada, and Content Warning.

My favorite types of games to play on my own are platformers (Cuphead, Celeste, Hollow Knight), puzzles (Paper Trail), and narrative-based (Gris, Stray, Papers Please).

Tabletop Games

My family and I always play Korean traditional games like 윷놀이 (Yutnori) and 화투 (Hwatu). We also have a collection of board games including, of course, Scrabble and Monopoly.

I also love doing puzzles, both in-person and online.

Dance

I have danced since I was 3 years old! I have trained in many styles including ballet, jazz, and tap, but discovered my favorite style (hip hop) in university. I was the vice-president of Case Western Reserve University's hip hop dance club, Undivided. I also competed with their competition team and we won first place at Dance Stomp Shake and second place at Reign or Shine. Watch our routine here!

Currently, I am part of RIT's Hip Hop Dance Collective and Velocity Urban Dance Crew.

Reading

My favorite genres are realistic fiction and magic realism. Add me on Goodreads!

Crafts

I like making miniature rooms, crocheting, and making personalized cards for my friends.
